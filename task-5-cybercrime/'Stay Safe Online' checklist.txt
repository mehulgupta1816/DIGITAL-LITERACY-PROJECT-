Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

Do’s
Use strong, unique passwords for each account (include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols).
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
Verify URLs carefully before clicking on links, especially in emails or messages claiming urgency.
Download apps only from trusted platforms like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
Keep your phone and laptop updated with the latest security patches.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts or making payments.
Limit the personal information you share online, including phone number, address, and college details.
Be cautious when interacting with unknown people online; verify identity before trusting.

UPI and Financial Safety
Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone. Apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm will never ask for it.
Carefully check “collect/request money” notifications; approving them sends money instead of receiving it.

Reporting Cybercrime
Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 immediately in case of financial fraud.
